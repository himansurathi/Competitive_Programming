                      Venki • 3 years ago

                      The "Optimal Substructure Property" also called as "principle of optimality". In order to apply DP, solution to the problem should satisfy principle of optimality. However, determining the Principle of Optimality is little difficult task, hope some of the upcoming examples will clarify.

                      For example finding optimal solution to one sub-instance may prevent choosing optimal solution to another instance, i.e. the optimal instances are not independent.
